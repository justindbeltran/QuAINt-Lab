Quantum and AI Networks (QuAINt) Lab

Exploring the next-generation of network problems, quantum systems, and artificial intelligence.

About Us

The QuAINt Lab was founded by Professors Dan Rubenstein and Vishal Misra at Columbia University. While the lab's foundations were built on pioneering research in classical computer networks and multimedia systems, the group has recently pivoted to address the most pressing challenges in emerging technologies.

Current research focus spans quantum computing for solving complex classical network problems, designing the architecture and protocols for a future Quantum Internet, solving distributed network challenges for large-scale AI machines, and advancing the theory and application of synthetic control in network environments.
